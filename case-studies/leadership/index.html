Leadership, strategy & collaboration

Not a single project, but the through-line across my work: how I lead, set direction, and help a team move together.

Shipping products is great. What I find even more rewarding is helping others grow.
Leadership

Helping designers grow, on my team and beyond it. 10+ mentored across Bukalapak and ADPList.

  • Mentoring junior product designers at Bukalapak and on ADPList, plus student teams for GEMASTIK 2018 and Google Design Sprint courses.
  • Sharing the craft: workshops on Figma prototyping with variables and service blueprints, a framework for the design process, and curated UX laws.
  • Speaking and writing: Bukalapak Roundup 7, the Telkomsel UI/UX Clinic, and Medium.
Strategy

Turning business strategy into roadmaps a team can build, through facilitation rather than decree.

  • Translating business strategy into product roadmaps.
  • Customer journey mapping, and service blueprints across apps and operations.
  • Presenting design with an evidence-based framework.
  • Leading the USP workshop for the SmartSari expansion.
  • Improving product clarity with action-based mapping.
Collaboration

Building the systems that let a team move together, not just my own screens.

  • One design system unifying 19+ products for consistency.
  • Modular design templates that sped delivery up to seven times.
  • Figma as a single source of truth, so every squad works from the latest design.
  • Onboarding guidelines for Mitra Bukalapak, reused across 9+ projects.
  • One-page documentation for visibility, and asynchronous meetings that protect focus.
