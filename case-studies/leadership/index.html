Leadership, strategy & collaboration

Not a single project, but the through-line across my work. Shipping is good. What I find even more rewarding is helping the people around me get better, turning strategy into something a team can actually build, and making collaboration work instead of just happen.

Shipping products is great. What I find even more rewarding is helping others grow.
Leadership

Over two years I've mentored 10+ designers, inside Bukalapak and through ADPList, along with students just starting out. I share what I've learned wherever it's useful:

  • Mentoring: junior product designers at Bukalapak and on ADPList, plus student teams for GEMASTIK 2018 and Google Design Sprint courses.
  • Sharing the craft: workshops on Figma prototyping with variables and service blueprints, a framework for the design process, and curated UX laws.
  • On stage and in writing: speaker at Bukalapak Roundup 7 and the Telkomsel UI/UX Clinic, and writing on Medium.
Strategy

Design is a collective effort. I help convert business strategy into product roadmaps by facilitating team-based discussions, design sprints, co-creation, and journey mapping, rather than deciding in a corner and hoping it lands.

  • Translating business strategy into product roadmaps.
  • Customer journey mapping, and service blueprints across apps and operations.
  • Presenting design with an evidence-based framework.
  • Leading the unique selling proposition workshop for the SmartSari expansion.
  • Improving product clarity with action-based mapping.
Collaboration

Efficient communication makes or breaks a team, and I try to practice what I preach. Beyond my own screens, I build the scaffolding that lets a team move together:

  • One design system unifying 19+ virtual products for consistency.
  • Modular design templates that sped delivery up to seven times.
  • Figma as a single source of truth, so every squad works from the latest design.
  • Onboarding guidelines for Mitra Bukalapak, reused across 9+ projects.
  • One-page documentation for visibility, and asynchronous meetings that protect focus.
